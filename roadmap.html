Roadmap

What we're building, what we're thinking about, and what you told us you need. Last updated April 2026.

01 · now
In progress · Q2 2026

What we're building right now.

ZopDay

ZopNight for GCP-native infrastructure. Full CDCR loop for Google Cloud, Compute Engine, GKE, Cloud SQL, and BigQuery waste detection and remediation.

~60% complete

Terraform Provider v2

Expanded resource coverage: tagging policies, alert rules, and recommendation dismissals. Drift detection for ZopNight configurations managed via Terraform.

~45% complete

Cost Anomaly ML Model

Replacing our rule-based anomaly detection with a per-account ML model. Trained on your historical spend patterns, not industry averages. Fewer false positives, faster detection.

~70% complete

AWS Marketplace

Self-service ZopNight subscription through AWS Marketplace. Consolidated billing through your AWS account. No separate invoice. Private offers for Enterprise.

~80% complete · expected May 2026

Multi-Region HA (Self-Hosted)

High-availability deployment option for self-hosted ZopNight Enterprise. Active-active across two regions with automatic failover and zero-downtime updates.

~35% complete
02 · next
Planned · Q3 2026

What's coming after this.

Azure Cost Management Integration

Deep integration with Azure Cost Management APIs for richer billing data, commitment coverage analysis, and reservation utilization reporting.

Kubernetes Namespace Cost Allocation v2

Accurate cost allocation at the Kubernetes namespace and workload level. Node cost attribution with shared resource handling for cluster-wide components.

Mobile Notifications

iOS and Android push notifications for critical findings, schedule alerts, and large anomalies. Configurable per-user thresholds and quiet hours.

DORA Metrics Integration

Correlate deployment frequency and change failure rate with cost anomalies. Surface the cost impact of incidents and post-deploy infrastructure changes automatically.

SOC 2 Type II Renewal

Annual SOC 2 Type II audit and report renewal. New controls added for MCP Server access and AI-assisted remediation approval workflows.

03 · later
Exploring · Q4 2026+

The bigger bets.

ZopCloud Unified Platform

A single pane of glass for ZopNight and ZopDay. Unified inventory, cross-cloud findings, and a single showback view across AWS, Azure, and GCP.

GCP Marketplace

Self-service ZopNight subscription through the Google Cloud Marketplace. Consolidated billing and private offers for Enterprise customers.

Oracle Cloud Support

OCI instance inventory, idle resource detection, and cost showback for Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. Early access program launching Q4 2026.

Advanced Forecasting

ML-driven spend forecasting at the account, team, and resource level. Budget vs. forecast alerts with automatic escalation policies.

Custom Policy Engine

Define your own waste detection rules and remediation playbooks. OPA-compatible policy language. Run custom policies alongside ZopNight's built-in engine.

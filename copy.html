§ 00 · voice

Five principles.

Before you write a line, check it against these five. If it fails even one, rewrite. Everything below in the dictionary obeys them.

01 · outcome
Outcome over feature.

Lead with the number the customer cares about. Not the technology that produced it. “$38K reclaimed”, not “advanced rightsizing engine”.

02 · direct
Direct. Declarative.

Short sentences. Active verbs. Concrete numbers. No hedging. No "might", "could", "possibly". We either ship it or we don't.

03 · claim
Name the category.

We don't enter categories, we name them. “The Continuous CloudOps platform.” “The CDCR loop.” “The three-numbers dashboard.”

04 · numbers
Numbers are punctuation.

Every claim is followed by a concrete figure. “5 min to connect. 7 min to first fix. 42% median cut.”

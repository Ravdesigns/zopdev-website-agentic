how we work

Three things we'll be annoyingly consistent about.

If you read these and roll your eyes, we're probably not for you. That's fine. These aren't aspirational.

01

Operators write the code.

The people who built the systems we're replacing now build ours. Every PM has been on-call. Every founder has paid an AWS bill they couldn't explain. We hire for that scar tissue.

02

Ship in days, learn in weeks.

Most features ship in under 5 days from idea to prod. We delete more than we keep. If your last team's deploy cycle was monthly, this will be a culture shock, in a good way.

03

Boring stuff matters.

Healthcare. 401k match. Real expense budgets. PTO that's actually used. We don't pretend ping-pong tables substitute for compensation.
